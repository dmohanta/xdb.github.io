Welcome to locateme

By Sanchita Roy and Debashish Mohanta

In case of distress, locateme can be used to locate a person using the gps location provided from the cell phone.

It uses the gps information from the cell phone and using whatsapp api's sends these information to predecided contacts in whatsapp periodically.

The receiver app will map the gps location of both sender and receiver onto google maps for tracking.
